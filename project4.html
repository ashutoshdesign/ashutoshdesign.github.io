Technothlon Exam App

There was a large communication gap between participants and Team Technothlon, IIT Guwahati. How might we then make participants more informed and updated about Technothlon, the international school championship?

This project was done in collaboration with Team Technothlon IIT Guwahati during January 2017.

Project Overview

"Technothlon Exam App" is an attempt at bridging the communication gap that exists between the organisers and the participants. My role in the project included conceptualising, wireframing, visual design and front-end developement.

Tools used

Learnings Recap

This was the first time I had made a mobile application. I learnt how to make a simple information architecture, wireframes and user interface of the app. Here I also got exposure of Google Material Design guidelines and have also tried to follow it throughout the app. Through this app, I honed my front-end development skills i.e. HTML/CSS, JavaScript and jQuery.
