Hello!
I'm Ashutosh Agrawal,
Senior Interaction Designer at D. E. Shaw India.

There’s a phrase in Sanskrit that has been the guiding motto in my life - 'There’s no bigger source of happiness than knowledge.’ This keeps me learning, experimenting, growing. This keeps me- curious!

Having grown up with a healthy curiosity for behavioral aspects of human beings and a predilection for advanced tech, I decided to study ‘Design’ at the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IITG). During my undergraduate years, I honed my ‘design thinking’ through theory and practice. I took every opportunity to gain exposure to various branches of design through electives, workshops, projects and internships.

In July 2019, I joined D. E. Shaw India where I design enterprise user experiences for a wide range of users. I have spearheaded UX efforts for 9 major projects employing the user-centered design process, notable among them being the firm's data visualziation and analytics tool.

When I’m not working, I am mostly outside- playing either Badminton, Squash, Cricket, or Waterpolo. I have represented my school and college at various regional and national level tournaments in these sports. Going on long motorcycle rides is another thing that I enjoy. It takes me to new places, meet new people and create new experiences. Every weekend, I cook numerous delicacies from diverse corners of my country.

My journey so far has motivated me to pursue a professional career at the intersection of HCI and healthcare. As a UX Researcher, I want to explore future human-comupter interfaces that enhance people’s mental, social, and physical well-being.
