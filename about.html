Hello!
I'm Ashutosh Agrawal,
a prefinal year undergraduate design student at Indian Institute of Technology, Guwahati.

Born in a small town in Chhattisgarh, I'm a Delhi Public School, Vindhyanagar pass-out, who managed to get into one of the premier institutions for design and engineering in our country.

Most recently, I spent a couple of months interning at 1mg.com, India's largest health platform and one of the top 10 start-ups of 2017 in India. It was a completely transforming experience for the designer in me. Now, I'm getting hold of what design is and would love exploring the field more, learning new technologies and skills on the way. I'm always on a lookout for new challenges.

Apart from design, I'm a sports enthusiast with love for Squash and Badminton. I have represented my institute at Inter-IIT Sports Meet and am currently the Institute Squash Secretary. I love road trips especially when I'm behind the wheel.
