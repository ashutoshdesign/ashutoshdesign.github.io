Resonance

"Urghh! What I'm gonna do for the rest of my flight! My phone's dead!" This situation sure is ugly. How might we then enable smartphone users to charge their phones on the move?

This project was done as a part of the Student Interaction Design Challenge 2016 during December 2017.

Project Overview

"Resonance" is a wearable for charging smartphones and other such electronics using "sound" from the surroundings as the source of energy. The video below explains the concept-
