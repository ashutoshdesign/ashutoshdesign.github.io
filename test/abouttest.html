Hello! I'm Ashutosh Agrawal, a

undergraduate prefinal year design

student at Indian Institute of

Technology, Guwahati.

Born in a small town in Chhattisgarh, I'm a Delhi Public School, Vindhyanagar pass-out, who managed to get into one of the premier institutions for design and engineering in our country.

Most recently, I spent a couple of months interning at 1mg.com, India's largest health platform and one of the top 10 start-ups of 2017 in India. It was a completely transforming experience for the designer in me. Now, I'm getting hold of what design is and would love exploring the field more, learning new technologies and skills on the way. I'm always on a lookout for new and diverse challenges.

Apart from design, I'm a sports enthusiast with love for Squash and Badminton.
